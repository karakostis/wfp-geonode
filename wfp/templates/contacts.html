WFPGeoNode is the WFP's corporate web application for creating and sharing geospatial data and maps.

It has been designed by the Emergency Preparedness and Response Unit (WFP OMEP) to empower non-GIS experts to easily produce maps with information already available in GeoNode or uploaded through a very simple process.

If you want to learn more about the WFPGeoNode or experience any problem while using it please contact omep.gis@wfp.org and an expert will get back to you soon.
